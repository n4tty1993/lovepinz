# Product Vision

## Product Name

Custom Magnetic Pins – D2C website

## Positioning

"The easiest way to create custom magnetic pins — no holes, no fabric damage."

## Core Platform Features

The online platform must allow consumers to perform the following actions seamlessly:

- **Upload Design:** Users can upload their own artwork or design for the pin.
- **Customize Options:** Users can select the size and finish of their pins.
- **See Real-Time Pricing:** The total price updates instantly based on customization choices and quantity.
- **Place Orders Instantly:** A complete e-commerce checkout process.
- **Approve Digital Proof:** Before the order is sent to production, the user must approve a digital proof of their final design.
