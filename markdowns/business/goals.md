# Business and Product Goals

## Business Goals

- **Enable Fully Automated Ordering:** Eliminate the manual quote process entirely.
- **Reduce Friction in Customization:** Make the design and ordering process as smooth as possible for the user.
- **Increase AOV:** Encourage larger orders through quantity and size upgrades.
- **Maintain Professional Proof Control:** Ensure a digital proof is approved by the customer before any production begins.

## Product Goals

- **Intuitive Builder Experience:** The tool for customizing pins should be easy and straightforward to use.
- **Transparent Pricing:** Pricing should update in real-time as the user makes changes to their order.
- **Fast Mobile Performance:** The website must be fast and responsive on all mobile devices.
- **Secure Artwork Handling:** User-uploaded designs and artwork must be handled securely.
