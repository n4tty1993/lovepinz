# Business Overview

## Company Background

We are a team of three co-founders with a strong background in e-commerce strategy, development, and growth. We operate as hands-on builders and partners, not as a traditional digital agency. Our focus is on helping brands launch and scale high-performing online stores.

Our combined expertise spans:
- Store architecture
- Conversion-focused UX
- Payment and logistics integrations
- Scalable customization workflows

## Business Model

We collaborate directly with merchants in the Custom Magnetic Pins industry to create reliable e-commerce platforms.

- **Product:** Custom Magnetic Enamel Pins
- **Market:** United States
- **Model:** Direct-to-consumer (D2C)
- **Minimum Order:** 25 units
- **Base Pricing:** Starts at ~$3 per unit
